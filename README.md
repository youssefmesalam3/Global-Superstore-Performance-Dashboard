# 📊 Global Superstore Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI Dashboard** built using the Global Superstore dataset to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends. The dashboard provides actionable insights through dynamic visualizations and KPI tracking, enabling better business decision-making.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall business performance.
* Monitor key sales and profit metrics.
* Identify top-performing categories, regions, states, cities, and customers.
* Evaluate customer purchasing behavior.
* Track sales trends over time.

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Sales:** $2.3M
* **Total Profit:** $286.4K
* **Profit Margin:** 12.5%
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 38K
* **Total Orders:** 5K
* **Total Customers:** 793

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1️⃣ Sales Performance Overview

* Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
* Sales by Category and Segment
* Profit by Category
* Quantity Distribution by Category
* Quantity Distribution by Segment

### 2️⃣ Regional Analysis

* Top 10 States by Sales
* Top 5 Cities by Sales
* Sales Distribution by Region
* Quantity Distribution by Region
* Top 5 Cities by Quantity

### 3️⃣ Customer Analysis

* Top 10 Customers by Sales
* Top 10 Customers by Quantity
* Top 10 Customers by Number of Orders
* Customer Performance Insights

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Technology generated the highest sales and profit among all categories.
* The West region achieved the highest sales performance.
* California was the top-performing state by sales.
* New York City recorded the highest sales volume among all cities.
* A small group of customers contributed significantly to overall revenue.

## 📂 Dataset

* Customers
* Products
